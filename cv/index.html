Research Areas

  • My research focuses on developing methods and computational approaches for the examination of forensic evidence, mainly focused on pattern evidence.
  • I am also interested in the interrelationship of mutliple types of evidence and how forensic analysis can be extended beyond the traditional case-by-case usage through forensic intelligence frameworks and Bayesian networks.
  • Keywords: Forensic Pattern Evidence; Forensic Intelligence; Evidence Interpretation; Bayesian Statistics; Criminal Case Assessment.
